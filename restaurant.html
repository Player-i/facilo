Like a fish in the water

ABOVE AND BELOW THE SURFACE

Paperfish Sushi is a contemporary Tokyo-inspired izakaya with masterfully crafted Nikkei cuisine. Here, artfully crafted dishes resplendent with Japanese elegance are combined with the seasonal tastes of Peru in a welcoming, modern-meets-traditional environment. Enter through the red torii gatessymbolically marking the transition from the mundane to the sacredonto an open-aired terrace adorned with draping plants, Japanese street signs, and a sea of soft-lit paper lanterns. Inside, a clean palette of natural materials and wood accents offset the pink petals of an enchanting cherry blossom tree-lined dining room. Come test these waters where we dive into the finest seasonal ingredients invite a warm, social dining experience with a mix of small and main plates perfect for sharing.
